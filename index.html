Herne Bay Basketball Club was established over 50 years ago and was a founding member of the East Kent Basketball Association in 1961, which operates the local basketball competitions in the East Kent area.

Based at The Bay Sports Arena in Herne Bay, the club is managed by a group of dedicated volunteers led by Club Chairman Brian Steele. We currently provide basketball oppertunities for players at both a Junior and Senior level.
